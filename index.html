LadyBug-Man - My Work & Projects

Hey there, I'm LadyBug-Man and In my spare time, I like to make and share things, And this is the place where you can find all of it. A few of my favourite types of projects include:

  • Music
  • Art
  • Electronics
  • Games
  • Coding

I have been making games on and off for the better part of 15 years, Mostly recreating mechanics I saw in games I loved and wanted to figure out how developers may have gone about it. I used Unity3d for this and learned a lot, But now I am fully devoted to learning the Godot environment lately.

Learning to code through video games led me to discover Electronics and how to code micro-controllers like the Arduino. I spent many years making random projects for fun and my own home convenience. Even going so far to make a very early home automation assitant I called "PIR-Ce" (Percy). This was before smart home devices were common.

In 2023 I begun learning how to make/produce my own music. What started as an endeavour to make simple ChipTunes for a project I was working on at the time turned into a newfound love and creative pursuit.

And very recently I delved into the craft of websites. Taking me back to my highschool days of making projects in Adobe Dreamweaver (RIP). Re-learning HTML and CSS since has been surprsingly difficult but in the best way as I love figuring code out, plus integrating the little Java I knew has been fun!
